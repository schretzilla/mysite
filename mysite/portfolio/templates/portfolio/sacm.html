Asset Data Warehouse

November/2015

The Service Asset and configuration project I was put on during my time on the Enterprise Systems Management team. I used SSMS jobs to collect data from multipe applicaitons throught the company's applicaiton portfolio. In order to better visualize the data I built a fully interactive dashboards using Splunk. Using the dashboards I built directors and managers were able to easily visualize how our assets were distributed throughout the company and determin if specific PC support groups were overburdened.

I lead many presentations and demos to ISD leadership in order to show them what these dashboards could do for them.

Tools:

  • Microsoft SQL Database
  • SSMS
  • Splunk
