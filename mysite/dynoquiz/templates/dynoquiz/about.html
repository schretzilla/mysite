About Quiztro

History

In 2013 I was asked to develop a website for a clinical research lab. They wanted a way that they could ask questions to their patients and record their responses locally to avoid any issues with HIPAA. With little experience involving how to build a websites I went about building them what I could. The end result was a very static PHP site with a MySql DB. The app did the job but it didn't allow test administrators to edit the questions or choices of their tests without knowing how to edit and deploy code.

The poor quality of my work still bothers me however I'm' trying to learn from the experience. Since then, when learning a new language or framework, my first capstone project is to build a quiz distribution app that doesn't require the administrator to know anything about web development to create/distribute/edit their quizzes. The app has little practical meaning now, but the CRUD operations are a good step in the right direction to mastering a new language/framework.

Tools:

  • Django 1.9
  • Python 2.7
  • AngularJS 1
  • Twitter Bootstrap 3
  • Django Rest Framework v3
  • PostgreSQL 9.2
  • Hosted using OpenShift (small gear)
